The online food delivery database management system consists of information regarding the orders that have been placed through the app specifying the attributes-customer name, customer unique Id,order status, name of restaurant from which the food was delivered, name of delivery boy, dishes that have been ordered , quantity of food ,customer feedback of delivery, customer feedback of restaurant and dishes, mode of payment.It also contains the personal details such as customer phone number, email address, address of delivery etc. The database clearly consists of all the necessary details of each and every delivery that had been made through the app.
